Got some eggs on toast

Eggs on toast is a classic and versatile meal that can be enjoyed at any time of the day. To prepare this dish, start by toasting slices of bread to your desired level of crispiness. Meanwhile, heat a pan and crack open some fresh eggs, cooking them to your preferred level of doneness - whether that's sunny-side up, over easy, or scrambled. Once the eggs are ready, gently place them on top of the toast, seasoning with salt, pepper, and any additional toppings you desire, such as avocado slices, cherry tomatoes, or a sprinkle of parsley. The combination of warm, runny yolk mixing with the crunchy toast creates a comforting and delicious experience that is sure to satisfy your hunger and taste buds.


So for this recipe all you need is some of this:
  • Eggs

  • Toaster
  • Olive Oil
  • Seasoning for taste

and after that all you have to do is follow these simple steps plain and easy:
  1. Fry eggs in saucepan with olive oil on it all heated up
  2. Toast some bread in the toaster (DONT EAT THE TOASTER)
  3. Put the fried egg on the toast plain and simple
